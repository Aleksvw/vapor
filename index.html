Why Invest in Vapor Blasting?

Vapor blasting (also known as wet blasting) is the modern, environmentally friendly alternative to traditional sandblasting. It combines water, compressed air, and fine abrasive media to create a superior surface finish with no dust pollution.

Our business will provide specialized cleaning services for automotive, marine, industrial, and restoration applications with superior results that command premium pricing.

Sample vapor blasting machine cleaning an engine block

Advantages Over Sandblasting

Eco-Friendly

No dust pollution or hazardous silica. Uses 90% less abrasives than dry blasting.

Superior Finish

Creates a peened, polished surface perfect for restoration projects.

Versatile

Safe for aluminum, soft metals, plastics, and delicate parts that sandblasting would damage.
